# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** {{last_updated}}

This Cookie Policy is drafted pursuant to the [Italian Data Protection Authority Provision of 10 June 2021](https://www.garanteprivacy.it/web/guest/home/docweb/-/docweb-display/docweb/9677876) and EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by the browser. They fall into:

- **Technical cookies (necessary):** essential for site functionality, do not require consent.
- **Analytics cookies:** measure site usage in aggregate form.
- **Marketing cookies:** advertising profiling, require consent.

## 2. Cookies used

### Technical cookies (always active)

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `sb-access-token` | Supabase authentication | Session |
| `sb-refresh-token` | Session refresh | 7 days |
| `cookie-consent` | User cookie preferences | 6 months |
| `csrf-token` | CSRF protection | Session |

### Analytics cookies (require consent)

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics 4 | Aggregate statistics | 13 months |
| `_vercel_analytics` | Vercel Analytics | Performance RUM | 24h |

### Marketing cookies (require consent)

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `_fbp` | Meta Pixel | Retargeting | 3 months |
| `li_sugr` | LinkedIn Insight | Conversion tracking | 90 days |

## 3. Consent management

On first visit we show a banner allowing you to:

1. **Accept all** cookies
2. **Reject** non-necessary cookies
3. **Customise** preferences by category

Consent is recorded with timestamp, policy version, selected preferences. You can modify at any time from [/account/privacy](/account/privacy).

## 4. Google Consent Mode v2

We implement Google Consent Mode v2 with **denied** defaults for ad_storage, ad_user_data, ad_personalization, analytics_storage. Google tags fire only after explicit consent.

## 5. How to disable cookies

You can disable cookies from your browser settings:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge)

Disabling technical cookies may impair site functionality.

## 6. Non-EU transfers

Some providers (Google, Meta) may transfer data to the US. Transfers are based on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework or Standard Contractual Clauses.

See [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) section 7 for exercising GDPR rights.
